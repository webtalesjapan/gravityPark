SWINGS

Let gravity send you on an intense rush through the open air. Visit the the Nevis Swing – the World’s Most Famous Swing. Forwards, backwards, upside down or tandem with a friend – however you do it, the adrenaline rushes are next level. Or for something a little different try the Ledge Swing high above the heart of Queenstown. If the Swing won’t take your breath away, the view will.
