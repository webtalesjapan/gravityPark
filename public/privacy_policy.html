The website https://gravitypark.jp uses ‘cookies’ to track certain information and behavior on the site so that we can improve the quality of our website and improve our own marketing performance.

What are cookies?
Cookies are small files which are stored on a user's computer. They are designed to hold a modest amount of data specific to a website and can be accessed either by the web server or the client computer. This allows the server to deliver a page tailored to a particular user, or the page itself can contain some script which is aware of the data in the cookie and so is able to carry information from one visit to the website (or related site) to the next.

On our website, the cookies stores information about which activity, the number of customers, the date and time. We may collect cookies to allow us to better understand our customers through data analysis which enables us to target advertisements according to your interests. Importantly collecting cookies is not the same as collecting Personal Data; cookies will not collect personal information such as your name, email address, postal address, or telephone number. Importantly collecting cookies is not the same as collecting Personal Data; cookies will not collect personal information such as your name, email address, postal address, or telephone number.

Our website may include code snippets provided by social media companies such as Facebook and Instagram that can sense if you are already logged in to a given social media account so you can easily share our content with other social media users via that account. These code snippets read cookies set previously by the social media company while you are logged in and using those services. We can not access any of your Personal Data via those cookies.

As is normal on most websites we use Google Analytics to aggregate the usage data of our website visitors to compile reports and help us improve our website. We use Google Analytics to count the number of unique visitors to a web page or to the purchase engine to record where and when the traffic to our website comes from. Google Analytics uses Cookies to gather this information.

Can I block cookies if I wish to not share the information?
By default most web browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Internet Explorer) accept cookies. However, most also allow you to either block them or to be notified of their presence. Instructions on how to do this are in the privacy or help documentation of your browser. Please understand that blocking cookies may affect your ability to purchase activity through our website.
