Canyon Fly

The Canyon Fly of Gravity Park is a smooth fast ride across the canyon; one of Japan's scariest ziplines. It is 100m across the Arakawa river and 50 meters above the river. Are you scared of heights? Then we recommend you to not look down while flying across the canyon.Two native Japanese eagles - Tombi - live in a nest together in a large Kiyaki tree over looking the Canyon fly. If you are lucky they will be flying while you are flying ' Free as a bird '.
Even in the mandatory full body harness it really feels like flying.

IMPORTANT

AGE

You must be at least 15 years old.

WEIGHT

You need to be a minimum of 36 Kgs and if you are over 100kg please let the Operator know.

HEIGHT

You must be at lease 140 cm tall to fit our safety harnesses.

DURATION

Please plan on being on site for 90 minutes.
If you have a confirmed booking please arrive 20 minutes before your booking time to in order to complete the checkin process on time.
Please arrive on time, if you are late you may have to wait for an opening in the schedule or worst case, your booking will be cancelled.

CLOTHING

Please make sure you have walking shoes.
Open-toed or high heels are not allowed as they can be dangerous on the walk.
We will have shoes available for rent if necessary.
Please wear clothes that will accommodate a full body harness, (legs, waist and shoulders)
