By CAR
From TOKYO

A1: Via Kanetsu Expressway · Hanazono IC to Route 140 Route,
A2: Via Kanetsu Expressway - transfer to the KenOdo highway then exit at Hidaka Sayama. get onto route 299 and take it to the heart of Chichibu.
Turn left at route 140 and follow it to Mitsumineguchi station.
B: Via Chuo Expressway - transfer to the KenOdo highway then exit at Hidaka Sayama. get onto route 299 and take it to the heart of Chichibu.
Turn left at route 140 and follow it to Mitsumineguchi station.

From YAMANASHI

Take the Central Expressway · Kofu Showa IC on Route 140
About 2.5 hours via Ganzaka Tunnel.
※ Ganzaka Tunnel is a toll road.

By TRAIN or SHINKANSEN
From IKEBUKURO Station

Seibu Railway · Limited Express Red Arrow 1 hour 20 minutes to Seibu Chichibu Station. Then catch the express bus (No 5) to Mitsumineguchi station (25 minutes)


From HACHIOJI Station

Catch the JR Hachiko line to Higashi-Hanno station. At Higashi-Hanno change to Seibu Line to Seibu-Chichibu station. Then catch the express bus (No 5) to Mitsumineguchi Station.


From UENO Station

Take the JR Takasaki line to Kumagaya station, change from Kumagaya station to Chichibu Railway and take it to the last stop - Mitsumineguchi station.
