CANCELLATION POLICY


Before moving forward, kindly make sure you understand our Cancellation Policy.
Gravity Park has a strict Cancellation Policy, irrespective, of the adventure acitivity.
Any booking cancellation will be charged according to the following rules.
Withing 24 hours before booking 100%
Between 25 hours to 48 hours before the booking 50%
Before 48 hours 0%

We, at Gravity Park, allow our customers to change the date of their activity once, for free of cost. Kindly note, once you change the date of the activity, 100% cancellation policy will be applied in case of any further cancellation.
