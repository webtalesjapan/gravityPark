Our Location

Chichibu is an amazing tear drop shaped valley close to Tokyo. Over 5,000,000 visitors come to Chichibu every year to enjoy food, outdoor activities, flowers and temples. Gravity Park is situated at the head of the Valley in a National Geo Park that offers an array of places to see and things to do.
