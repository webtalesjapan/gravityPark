Medical Considerations

You must be in good health.
Before all the activities make sure you are well rested.
Tell the staff if you have high blood pressure, heart conditions, epilepsy, fragile skin, fractures or dislocations, asthma, or any other conditions you are unsure about. Read the medical board at reception and then inform us of any that apply. Anyone having a serious medical condition and/or has suffered a physical injury in the past and how it could be affected by the activity should first consult a doctor before booking. With a certain type of physical injury, the activity master at Gravity Park can advise you on a particular way to perform the activity for minimum stress on the injury.
Kindly understand, if you fail to report your medical condition prior to the activity, Gravity Park will not be held responsible for any problems.
Alcoholic beverages before the activity are highly prohibited.

Disabled & Impaired Customers

At Gravity Park we provide fun and safe activities for people who want an exciting environment to challenge themselves. Some of our activities have a few requirements and restrictions. Please contact our staff prior to booking so we can work out if the activity is suitable and how we can assist.
We are not medical professionals, so to ensure your safety and the safety of our staff we will require sign-off from a doctor or medical professional that the activity you choose will not harm you.
Our guidelines for Impaired participants give more details about physical requirements and the process to follow with your doctor.
